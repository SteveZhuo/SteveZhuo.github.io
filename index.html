Steve Weipeng Zhuo (卓伟鹏)

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science at Beijing Normal-Hong Kong Baptist University. Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Shueng-Han Gary Chan. Prior to that, I was an early founder in Pipe of Insight, a startup focused on online education using mobile devices, which raised angel round funding and series A financing. I obtained my BSc degree (double majored in Computer Science and Applied Math) and Master of Philosophy (MPhil) degree from HKUST. During my MPhil study, I was a visiting scholar in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Princeton University.

My research focuses on the design and application of graph neural networks (GNNs) on the internet of things (IoT) devices, including

  • Radio frequency signal analytics in smart city applications.
  • Graph neural networks and distributed training.
  • Human activity recognition using edge devices.
