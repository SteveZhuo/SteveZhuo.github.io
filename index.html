Steve Weipeng Zhuo (卓伟鹏)

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Shueng-Han Gary Chan. Prior to that, I was an early founder in Pipe of Insight, a startup focused on online education using mobile devices, which raised angel round funding and series A financing. I obtained my BSc degree (double majored in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics) and Master of Philosophy (MPhil) degree from HKUST. During my MPhil study, I was a visiting scholar in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Princeton University. I also work closely with Prof. Chul-Ho Lee and Prof. Sangtae Ha.

My research focuses on the design and application of graph neural networks (GNNs) on the internet of things (IoT) devices, including

  • Radio frequency (RF) signal learning in smart city applications.
  • Heterogeneous graph neural networks (embedding generation, node classification, etc).
  • User behavior study in indoor localization.
